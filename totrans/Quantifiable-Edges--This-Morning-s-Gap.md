Looks like the market is poised to gap lower by over 1% this morning. Having already been down two days in a row, risk/reward typically favors the long side from open to close in these situations. It's something I examined recently

[here](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2009/04/putting-large-gaps-down-into-context.html)

.

And any time there's a large gap about to happen you can always review the gap-related studies by simply using the
