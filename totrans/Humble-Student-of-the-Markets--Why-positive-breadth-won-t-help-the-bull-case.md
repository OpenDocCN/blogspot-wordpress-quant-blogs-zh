# Humble Student of the Markets: Why positive breadth won't help the bull case

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2018/08/why-positive-breadth-wont-help-bull-case.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2018/08/why-positive-breadth-wont-help-bull-case.html#0001-01-01)

**Mid-week market update**

: There was a lot of skepticism in response to my last post (see

[How many pennies left in front of the steamroller?](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.com/2018/08/20/how-many-pennies-are-left-in-front-of-the-steamroller/)

). Much of it was related to comments relating to positive market breadth.

I am in debt to

[Urban Carmel](https://twitter.com/ukarlewitz/status/994983882356277248)

 who pointed out back in May that the Advance-Decline Line tends to move coincidentally with the major market indices. Here is an updated five-year chart of the 52-week highs-lows, and the NYSE Advance-Decline Line. The peaks in both of these indicators have either been coincidental, or lagged the stock market peak.
