## Hiring again…

### January 10, 2008

I’m recruiting again, for a new project. The developers I hire may find themselves coding in C++, Java, C#, Python, SQL or JavaScript. But I’ll stick with my approach of just hiring strong C++ devs. From experience I know that a developer who’s mastered C++, STL and multithreading can tackle anything.
