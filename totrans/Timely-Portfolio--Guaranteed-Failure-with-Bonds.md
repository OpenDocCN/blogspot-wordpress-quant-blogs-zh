# Timely Portfolio: Guaranteed Failure with Bonds

> 来源：[http://timelyportfolio.blogspot.com/2011/04/guaranteed-failure-with-bonds.html#0001-01-01](http://timelyportfolio.blogspot.com/2011/04/guaranteed-failure-with-bonds.html#0001-01-01)

If we define failure as

> the inability to meet expectations

then bonds are guaranteed failures.

If we define failure in money management as

> losing money

then bonds are likely failures.
