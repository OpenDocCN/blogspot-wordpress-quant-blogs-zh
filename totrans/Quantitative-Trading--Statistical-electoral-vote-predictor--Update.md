# Quantitative Trading: Statistical electoral vote predictor: Update

> 来源：[http://epchan.blogspot.com/2008/06/statistical-electoral-vote-predictor.html#0001-01-01](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2008/06/statistical-electoral-vote-predictor.html#0001-01-01)

For readers who have been tracking the

[Gott and Colley](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2008/06/statistical-model-predicts-mccain.html)

presidential electoral vote prediction, they will notice a sudden switch over to a predicted Obama victory in the last few days. That is because polls from OH, VA and MO are now available -- surprising because the 3 states are not hitherto known for their Democratic leanings.

It seems to me that, after all, the stability of prediction at this early date is quite questionable due to the paucity of state polls, a point already made by Dr. Colley.
