## Office 2007 Development in Financial Markets

[Here](http://msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?culture=en-GB&eventid=1032301774)

Office 2007 Development in Financial Markets

25 July 2006 17:30 – 25 July 2006 20:30 GMT, London
Welcome Time: 17:00
Language: English

Credit Suisse
One Cabot Square
London London E14 4QJ
United Kingdom

General Event Information
Products: Office.

Recommended Audience: Developer.

“The 2007 Microsoft Office system, available now as a beta and due to be released at the end of 2006, provides a host of new capabilities for developers of financial systems, including server-based Excel spreadsheet, new XML file formats, and server-based workflow processing.

This evening event, hosted at Credit Suisse offices in Canary Wharf and focused on the needs of developers in the financial sector, will provide you with a useful introduction to these technologies so that you can start to take advantage of them as soon as possible”.

5pm: Arrival and Registration

5.30pm to 5.50pm: Introduce Office 2007 and the concept of server development

5.50pm to 6.05pm: Credit Suisse re their use of Office

6.05pm to 7.15pm: BI306 “Building Custom Solutions with Excel Services”

7.15pm to 7.30pm: Break

7.30pm to 8.30pm: FF301/2/3 (subset): “New Office XML File Formats”

8.30pm: Close
