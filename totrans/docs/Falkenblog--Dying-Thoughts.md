# Falkenblog: Dying Thoughts

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2011/10/dying-thoughts.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2011/10/dying-thoughts.html#0001-01-01)

Steve Jobs, a true mensch, left an estate worth around $6.5B.

[Nevertheless](http://www.nytimes.com/2011/10/07/technology/with-time-running-short-steve-jobs-managed-his-farewells.html?pagewanted=1&_r=1&hp)

:

> “Steve made choices,” Dr. Ornish said. “I once asked him if he was glad that he had kids, and he said, ‘It’s 10,000 times better than anything I’ve ever done.’ ”

I think those of us who love our kids are better off than childless billionaires. More importantly, the value of our children is 10,000 times more important than any financial fortune.
