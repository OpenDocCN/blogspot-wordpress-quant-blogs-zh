# Falkenblog: Crisis and Legislation

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2009/02/crisis-and-legislation.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2009/02/crisis-and-legislation.html#0001-01-01)

Rahm Emannuel recently stated that "You never want a serious crisis to go to waste." See

[here](http://vodpod.com/watch/1181749-rahm-emanuel-dont-waste-a-serious-crisis)

. This is not merely a left wing tactic, as even Milton Friedman noted that "Only a crisis actual or perceived produces real change." (the theme of Naomi Klein's

[Shock Doctrine](http://www.naomiklein.org/shock-doctrine/the-book)

). Thus, it's a political reality, and in this crisis, Democrats control the Presidency and Congress.

Clinton's welfare reforms are now

[effectively repealed](http://online.wsj.com/article/SB123422835499665849.html)

, and we have $30MM to help protect the

[Salt Marsh Harvest Mouse](http://www.mercurynews.com/peninsula/ci_11696283)

. But who cares, we need to do something now, and every dollar spent in Washington, through the multiplier, pays for itself!
