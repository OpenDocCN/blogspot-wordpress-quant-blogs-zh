## “As a developer..” I think a stand-up is a team meeting

I still don’t get why people find it so hard to understand the aim of a stand-up – its such a simple [ceremony](https://www.atlassian.com/agile/ceremonies).  If you can’t follow the concept of a stand-up as documented and understood after 10 years, then stop calling it a stand-up – its embarrassing for yourself, the team, and by-standers.  The whole point of a stand-up is to aid collaboration, and ensure as a TEAM you achieve the goals you undertook as a team for the [sprint](https://www.mountaingoatsoftware.com/agile/scrum/daily-scrum).
