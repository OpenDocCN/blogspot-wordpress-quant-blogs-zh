# Humble Student of the Markets: The miracle of Europe

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2017/04/the-miracle-of-europe.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2017/04/the-miracle-of-europe.html#0001-01-01)

Here in Canada, we are observing the 100 year commemoration of the participation of Canadian troops in the

[Battle of Vimy Ridge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Vimy_Ridge)

. While the Canadian Corp achieved its objectives of capturing the ridge, it was a typical battle of the First World War that left enormous casualties for both sides.

After the horrific human carnage of the First and Second World Wars, western Europe formed the European Coal and Steel Community (ECSC), which led to the European Economic Community (EEC). Thus the EU was born.

The political intent of the union was to bind France and Germany so tightly together that another major European conflict could not happen again. Despite the setback provided by Brexit, those political intentions have succeeded.
