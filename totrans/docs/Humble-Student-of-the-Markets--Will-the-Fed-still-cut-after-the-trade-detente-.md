# Humble Student of the Markets: Will the Fed still cut after the trade detente?

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2019/07/will-fed-still-cut-after-trade-detente.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2019/07/will-fed-still-cut-after-trade-detente.html#0001-01-01)

The results out of the Trump-Xi summit were slightly better than market expectations. Not only do we have a trade truce, a suspension of escalation, but Trump promised that American companies can sell to Huawei.

Now that we have achieved a detente of sorts, the CME's

[Fedwatch Tool](https://www.cmegroup.com/trading/interest-rates/countdown-to-fomc.html)

shows the market is still discounting a 100% likelihood of a quarter-point rate cut at the July FOMC meeting, and a 21.4% chance of a half-point cut.

Is this for real? Will the Fed disappoint the markets?
