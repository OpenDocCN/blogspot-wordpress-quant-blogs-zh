# VIX and More: VIX and More: The Month in Words

> 来源：[http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/11/vix-and-more-month-in-words.html#0001-01-01](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/11/vix-and-more-month-in-words.html#0001-01-01)

Anyone who has yet to experiment with [Wordle](http://www.wordle.net/) is missing out. First [brought to my attention](http://bigpicture.typepad.com/comments/2008/06/fun-with-wordle.html) by Barry Ritholtz at [The Big Picture](http://bigpicture.typepad.com/), Wordle is a site that lets users turn text into a word cloud, with a wide array of creative options.

Wordle can be used purely for fun, for analysis, or for any number of purposes. It might also have some value as an archival tool too.
