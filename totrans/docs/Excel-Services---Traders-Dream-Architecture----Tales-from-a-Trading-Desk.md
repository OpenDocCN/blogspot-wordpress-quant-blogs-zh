## Excel Services – Traders Dream Architecture?

Could Microsoft’s up-and-coming [Excel Services](http://blogs.msdn.com/excel/archive/2005/11/10/491392.aspx) (part of Office 12) be the dream solution to the spread sheet nightmare that often takes over trading desks? Anyone comes across any investment banks using [Excel Web Services](http://blogs.msdn.com/excel/archive/2005/11/17/493962.aspx)? EWS could possible be a little to restrictive for most traders – given the lack of [create and edit](http://blogs.msdn.com/excel/archive/2005/11/09/490926.aspx) 😦
