## Minimum Blockchain Strategy

Just in case anyone has missed a few recent data points, the minimum blockchain strategy for a bank is probably:

*   [R3](https://recode.net/2015/09/15/nine-of-the-worlds-biggest-banks-form-blockchain-partnership/) – common [standards](https://bitcoinmagazine.com/21939/nine-top-global-banks-pool-resources-fund-r3-develop-digital-currency-standards/) for blockchain technology
