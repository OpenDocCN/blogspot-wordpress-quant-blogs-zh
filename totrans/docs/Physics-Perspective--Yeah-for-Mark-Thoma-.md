# Physics Perspective: Yeah for Mark Thoma!

> 来源：[http://physicsoffinance.blogspot.com/2014/12/yeah-for-mark-thoma.html#0001-01-01](http://physicsoffinance.blogspot.com/2014/12/yeah-for-mark-thoma.html#0001-01-01)

How many economists go out of their way to examine in public what they got wrong in their past views, and what they learned by making such mistakes? I'd say the list is exceedingly short.

So three cheers for Mark Thoma, who does just that in

[an excellent column](http://www.thefiscaltimes.com/Columns/2014/12/02/Why-Next-Recession-Will-Be-Different)

in the Financial Times. This is how you inspire trust.
