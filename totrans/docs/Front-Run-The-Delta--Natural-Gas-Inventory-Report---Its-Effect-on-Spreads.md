# Front-Run The Delta: Natural Gas Inventory Report & Its Effect on Spreads

> 来源：[https://frontrunthedelta.blogspot.com/2011/08/natural-gas-inventory-report.html#0001-01-01](https://frontrunthedelta.blogspot.com/2011/08/natural-gas-inventory-report.html#0001-01-01)

Every Thursday at 9:30AM CST, the

[EIA](http://www.eia.gov/)

releases the weekly U.S. natural gas inventory

[report](http://ir.eia.gov/ngs/ngs.html)

.  To the right is a chart of the Jan12 and Feb12

[Henry Hub](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Hub)

natural gas

[contracts](http://www.cmegroup.com/trading/energy/natural-gas/natural-gas_contract_specifications.html)

and the corresponding spread between them.  The chart is from 9:25am - 9:35am this afternoon, Thursday, Aug. 25, 2011\.
