## Nominated to receive an MVP Award in July 2011

Received this this morning:

> Congratulations! You have been nominated to receive an MVP Award in July 2011
> 
> I’m pleased to confirm that you have been nominated to receive a Microsoft® Most Valuable Professional (MVP) award as part of the July 2011 award cycle. The MVP award and program was created by Microsoft to recognise the best and brightest from the technology communities around the world. MVPs are exceptional community leaders who come from a wide range of backgrounds; they are teachers, artists, doctors, engineers, as well as technologists, who actively share their high-quality, real-world technical expertise with the community and with Microsoft. With the MVP Award, we thank inspiring individuals that represent the voice of thousands in the community through the powerful and independent feedback they give us, and for helping our customers maximize the potential of their software.
> 
> MVP Award Technical Expertise: If you are successful, you will be recognised for your hard work in the area of Server Solutions.
