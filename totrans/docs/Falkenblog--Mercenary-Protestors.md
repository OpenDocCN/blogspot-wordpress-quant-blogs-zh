# Falkenblog: Mercenary Protestors

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2012/05/mercenary-protestors.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2012/05/mercenary-protestors.html#0001-01-01)

A friend of mine works here in Minneapolis, and lives in Chicago (6 hour drive, 1 hour flight).  Last Friday he noted his flight was filled with nurses.  He asked what was up, and found their union was paying for hotel, airfare and a per diem to join some Chicago protests the following day.

The nurses were rather

[visible](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/05/18/nato-summit-chicago-nurses_n_1526881.html)

at the NATO summit, officially calling for a

[tax on financial transactions](http://www.myfoxchicago.com/story/18553115/nurses-pre-nato-rally-expected-to-draw-thousands)

, among other things.  The nurses my friend spoke to had little interest or understanding of what they were protesting, just noting it was part of being in a union, and going to be fun.

Nurses, presumably, have strong opinions on 

[Tobin taxes](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tobin_tax)

. Who knew?
