# Quantitative Trading: The limitation of Sharpe ratio

> 来源：[http://epchan.blogspot.com/2009/02/limitation-of-sharpe-ratio.html#0001-01-01](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2009/02/limitation-of-sharpe-ratio.html#0001-01-01)

Just as one should not trust

[VaR](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2009/02/peril-of-var.html)

completely, one should also beware of high Sharpe ratio strategies. As this

[Economist article](http://www.economist.com/finance/displaystory.cfm?story_id=12948575)

pointed out, a strategy may have a high Sharpe ratio because it has so far been accumulating small gains quite consistently, but it could still be subject to a large loss when black-swan events strike.

Personally, I am more comfortable with strategies that do the opposite: those that seldom generate any returns, but always earn a large profit when financial catastrophes occur.
