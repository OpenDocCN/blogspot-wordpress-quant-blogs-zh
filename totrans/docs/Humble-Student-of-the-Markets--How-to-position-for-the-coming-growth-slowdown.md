# Humble Student of the Markets: How to position for the coming growth slowdown

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2023/04/how-to-position-for-coming-growth.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2023/04/how-to-position-for-coming-growth.html#0001-01-01)

The International Monetary Fund published its latest

[World Economic Outlook](https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO?page=1)

. It cut its global GDP growth estimate by 0.1% from 2.9% in January to 2.8%. More ominously, it issued a warning about a growing risk of recession in the advanced economies from financial instability risk from bank failures: “A hard landing — particularly for advanced economies — has become a much larger risk” 

In light of the risks of a substantial slowdown, how should investors position themselves?
