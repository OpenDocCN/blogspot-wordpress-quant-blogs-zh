# Timely Portfolio: Power of Sparklines in Advent Axys

> 来源：[http://timelyportfolio.blogspot.com/2012/01/power-of-sparklines-in-advent-axys.html#0001-01-01](http://timelyportfolio.blogspot.com/2012/01/power-of-sparklines-in-advent-axys.html#0001-01-01)

I recently discovered the amazing work of Fabrice Rimlinger at [http://sparklines-excel.blogspot.com](http://sparklines-excel.blogspot.com) and thought of what I believe is a novel way of using the Microsoft Excel integration with Advent Axys reports to potentially achieve a dashboard style report.  This would be far more visually compelling and informative than anything else I have seen in Axys.  For this report, we will just use the standard Axys Portfolio Summary Report and then an Excel macro [http://www.box.com/s/9jenb341qmv4iaoeh5uy](http://www.box.com/s/9jenb341qmv4iaoeh5uy "http://www.box.com/s/9jenb341qmv4iaoeh5uy").  Please also install the appropriate Excel add-in from [http://sparklines-excel.blogspot.com](http://sparklines-excel.blogspot.com).
