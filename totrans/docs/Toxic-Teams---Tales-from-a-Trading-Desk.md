## Toxic Teams

Todd Henry offers an interesting [read](http://www.accidentalcreative.com/teams/toxic-dealing-with-a-culture-of-blame/) on “Toxic: Dealing With A Culture Of Blame”.  On the gossip point mentioned in the article, I’d say gossip is sometimes also known as “water cooler chatter”, or “down the pub” conversations.  “A general lack of accountability on the team” isn’t uncommon based on Kerry Patterson’s recent [article](http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/crucial-conversations/201306/accountability-what-dysfunctional-teams-are-missing) “Accountability—What Dysfunctional Teams Are Missing”.  Which leads us to the responsibilities of the [team](http://blog.robbowley.net/responsibilities-of-a-team-leader/) leader, and thus essentially a [dysfunctional](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6dRKa700RaQ) team.
