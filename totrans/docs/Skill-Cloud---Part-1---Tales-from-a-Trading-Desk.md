## Skill Cloud – Part 1

Job Titles 😦  People get hung up about job titles.  They obsess about them.  The title an employee has is often never the one they want – they always wants a higher title.  Titles cause boxing of individual during resourcing discussions

As the [article](http://thenextweb.com/entrepreneur/2014/05/23/skill-cloud-companies-kill-job-titles) implies, skill clouds offer the meta data around a job title to allow the building of teams that together provide the overall skillsets to solve problems, and deliver solutions.
