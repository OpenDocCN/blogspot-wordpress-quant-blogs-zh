## Cyber Security – Notes

A few weeks ago, the Sunday Times included a CyberSecurity supplement, available online [here](https://www.forcepoint.com/sites/default/files/resources/files/report-cybersecurity-2019-sunday-times-en.pdf).  What follows are a few interested pointers:

*   [Human](https://www.forcepoint.com/product/ueba-user-entity-behavior-analytics) [Centered](https://www.forcepoint.com/sites/default/files/resources/files/transcript_making_shift_to_human_centered_security_en.pdf) Security
*   Phishing attempts is number one in terms of insider threat
*   Increase in awareness and training can lead to the [biggest](https://www.oracle.com/a/ocom/docs/cloud/cloud-threat-report-2019-infographic.pdf) improvement in security
*   Cloud – understand where your sensitive data is, assess access and sharing privileges.
*   Open source vulnerabilities create [serious](https://www.veracode.com/security/open-source-vulnerabilities) risks.
