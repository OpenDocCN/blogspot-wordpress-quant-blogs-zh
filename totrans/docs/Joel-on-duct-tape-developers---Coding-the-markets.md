## Joel on duct tape developers

### September 24, 2009

As usual [Joel hits the nail on the head](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/items/2009/09/23.html) in contrasting pragmatic, delivery focused “duct tape programmers” with perfection obsessed abstraction astronauts who never ship because they can’t stop polishing and honing. It’s those duct tape devs we’re always looking to hire for front office trading systems, cos time to market is everything in our game…
