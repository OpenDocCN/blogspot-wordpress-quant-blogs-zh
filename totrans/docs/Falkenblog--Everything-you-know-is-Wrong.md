Wearing horizontal stripes makes you look thinner, according to new research – contrary to widely held belief that vertical stripes are more flattering to the figure.

The discovery was

[presented](http://www.cosmosmagazine.com/news/2192/to-look-slim-wear-horizontal-not-vertical-stripes)

at the British Association for the Advancement of Science (BA) Festival of Science, held in Liverpool, England, last week.
