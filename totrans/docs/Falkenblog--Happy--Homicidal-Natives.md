You have to love anthropologists. When you see a backward, they see a beautiful authenticity--but only if it's non-Western (eastern Kentucky is just backward). Regarding the photograph of some tribesmen in remote Peru, indigenous tribes expert, José Carlos Meirelles,

[exalts](http://www.news.com.au/story/0,23599,23911279-23109,00.html)

:

> "When I saw them painted red, I was satisfied, I was happy," he said.

> "Because painted red means they are ready for war, which to me says they are happy and healthy defending their territory."
