# Quantitative Trading: A sea of pain

> 来源：[http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/12/sea-of-pain.html#0001-01-01](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/12/sea-of-pain.html#0001-01-01)

This Economist Magazine

[article](http://economist.com/finance/displaystory.cfm?story_id=10286619)

confirms my personal experience that value investing is in a sea of pain at the moment. The reasons are quite different from the last time (during the dotcom era) when value investing was in the doldrums. This time around, people are not full of euphoria about the prospects of growth stocks -- they are just getting increasingly gloomy of value stocks which seem to be getting cheaper by the minute.
