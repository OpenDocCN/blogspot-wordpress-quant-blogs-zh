# Falkenblog: New Paper on Low Risk Investing

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2010/04/new-paper-on-low-risk-investing.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2010/04/new-paper-on-low-risk-investing.html#0001-01-01)

My book,

[Finding Alpha](http://www.amazon.com/Finding-Alpha-Search-Return-Finance/dp/0470445904/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1243302335&sr=1-1)

, highlights that conventional, and really any intuitive measure of risk, is at best unrelated to return. At worst, it is negatively related to returns.

[Baker, Bradley, and Wurgle](http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=1585031)

r have a new, simple, paper on low risk equity portfolios.

This was pointed to me by Pim van Vliet, a quantitative researcher at Robeco. He has a nice presentation on Robeco's low risk portfolios

[here](http://mediazone.brighttalk.com/comm/Robeco/9bf798a708-13459-3021-14382)

, and an SSRN paper on low-risk equity portfolios

[here](http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=980865)
