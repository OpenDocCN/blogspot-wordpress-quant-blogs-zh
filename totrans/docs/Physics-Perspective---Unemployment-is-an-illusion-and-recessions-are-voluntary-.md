# Physics Perspective: "Unemployment is an illusion and recessions are voluntary"

> 来源：[http://physicsoffinance.blogspot.com/2012/03/unemployment-is-illusion-and-recessions.html#0001-01-01](http://physicsoffinance.blogspot.com/2012/03/unemployment-is-illusion-and-recessions.html#0001-01-01)

This blog  explores the potential for the transformation of economics and finance through the inspiration of physics and the other natural sciences.

If traditional economics has emphasized self-regulation and market equilibrium, the new perspective emphasizes the myriad positive feed backs that often drive markets away from equilibrium and cause tumultuous crashes and other crises.
