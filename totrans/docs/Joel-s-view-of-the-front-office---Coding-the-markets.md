## Joel’s view of the front office

### September 13, 2006

So we’re still in the middle of the recruiting round I alluded to earlier. [Joel on Software](http://www.joelonsoftware.com) is one of my regular reads, and I’ve followed his [recent posts on recruiting](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/SortingResumes.html). [One post](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/FieldGuidetoDevelopers.html) presents an amusingly stereotyped caricature of the relationship between developers and traders, and bank attitudes to technology. Scroll down to the bit about “**Use cool new technologies unnecessarily”:** I think Joel must have read Liar’s Poker. Needless to say, it’s not really like that…
