# Quantitative Trading: Emerging markets stocks vs. natural resource stocks

> 来源：[http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/10/emerging-markets-stocks-vs-natural.html#0001-01-01](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/10/emerging-markets-stocks-vs-natural.html#0001-01-01)

Emerging market stocks have been reaching new highs almost everyday (see this

[article](http://economist.com/finance/displaystory.cfm?story_id=9912520)

in the Economist magazine), and the natural resource sector has been on a tear as well. Given the giddy valuations of both sectors, which one is a better relative buy at this point? For those of you who have been following the IGE-EEM spread that I

[proposed](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2006/11/does-canada-belong-to-emerging-markets.html)

before, its value is at an all-time-low these days -- it was at -6.77 standard deviations. Given their historical cointegration, I wouldn't be surprised if it will revert to a more sane value in the near future.
