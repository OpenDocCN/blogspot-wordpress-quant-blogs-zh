## Knight Capital – Don’t Test In Production

Unclear of what Knight Capital’s testing policy is, but I suspect there will be [improvements](http://www.wallstreetandtech.com/electronic-trading/240004831) in this area after the $440 million [loss](http://finance.yahoo.com/news/analysis-knight-trading-loss-shows-023247909.html) in 45 minutes, coupled with improved monitoring.

> Outsiders questioned why Knight did not pre-test the new software more assiduously, and why the bad trades continued to be generated for more than half an hour, instead of being shut down by internal systems almost [immediately](http://finance.yahoo.com/news/analysis-knight-trading-loss-shows-023247909.html).
