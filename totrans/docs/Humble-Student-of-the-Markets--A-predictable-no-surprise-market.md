# Humble Student of the Markets: A predictable no surprise market

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2019/09/a-predictable-no-surprise-market.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2019/09/a-predictable-no-surprise-market.html#0001-01-01)

**Mid-week market update**

: Subscribers received an alert last Friday that I had turned tactically cautious on the market. So far, this has been a fairly predictable market with few surprises.

Rob Hanna at

[Quantifiable Edges](https://quantifiableedges.com/why-todays-fed-day-setup-may-not-be-as-bullish-as-most/)

documented how stock prices have been during FOMC days when the SPX closed at a 20-day high the day before. That's because the market had risen in anticipation of a positive announcement from the Fed, and the reaction is at best a coin toss.

Today's roller coaster market action was no surprise.
