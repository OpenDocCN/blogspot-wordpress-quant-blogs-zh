# Falkenblog: Our New Power Elite

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2010/01/our-new-power-elite.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2010/01/our-new-power-elite.html#0001-01-01)

As we move into our new era of greater government, there are many signs of the new regime. One is the great deference by risk managers to regulators, turning what was once a promising intellectual field into a subservient rationalization for whatever the regulators appear to want, conventional wisdom on steroids (ie, rigorous and common yet often irrelevant). Another is that rents in DC are

[approaching](http://online.wsj.com/article/SB126291211587420679.html)

those in New York.

But now, we have the new alpha male: übernerd

[Peter Orszag](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_R._Orszag)

. This guy probably experienced his share of

[weggies](http://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=weggies)

in junior high, but
