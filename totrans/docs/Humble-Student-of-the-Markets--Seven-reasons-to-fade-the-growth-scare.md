# Humble Student of the Markets: Seven reasons to fade the growth scare

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2021/07/seven-reasons-to-fade-growth-scare.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2021/07/seven-reasons-to-fade-growth-scare.html#0001-01-01)

It is astonishing to see the market narrative shift in the space of only a few months from "inflation is coming" to a growth scare. In late March, the 10-year Treasury yield topped at over 1.7% and the 2s10s yield curve was steepening. Today, the 10-year has decisively broken support and the yield curve is flattening, indicating fears of slowing economic growth.

In late May, I forecasted bond market price strength and called for a counter-trend rally in growth stocks (see

[What a bond market rally could mean for your investments](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.com/2021/05/29/what-a-bond-market-rally-could-mean-for-your-investments/)

) but the latest move in both yields and growth appear exhaustive. As evidence of the change in psychology, 

[Bloomberg](https://t.co/y7RY7HuUz8)

reported that put option premiums over calls on the 10-Year Treasury Note have vanished. Traders are now paying more for call options than put options.

Here are seven reasons why investors should fade the growth scare.
