# Falkenblog: Smart People are Nicer

> 来源：[http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2008/11/smart-people-are-nicer.html#0001-01-01](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2008/11/smart-people-are-nicer.html#0001-01-01)

So implies

[this study](http://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/11/smart-people-play-nice.php)

, where people who scored higher on IQ tests played "nice" on the first move in the iterated prisoner's dilemma game. Too bad, because this means groups of less intelligent people hurt themselves further by avoiding the positive gains from trade.
