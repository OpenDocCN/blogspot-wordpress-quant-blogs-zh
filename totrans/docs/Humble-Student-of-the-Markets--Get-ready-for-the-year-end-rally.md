**Mid-week market update:**

The Fed delivered a dovish pause today. In addition, Powell was given opportunities to push back with bearish scenarios, such as raising concerns over the recent risk-on rally as a sign that financial conditions are loosening, or the elevated levels of super-core inflation, but he declined to do so. It is becoming more and more evident that the rate hike cycle is over. The market is looking forward to rate cuts and it’s discounting cuts to begin in March and five quarter-point cuts in 2024.
