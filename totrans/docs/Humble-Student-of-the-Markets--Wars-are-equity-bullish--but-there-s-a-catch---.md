# Humble Student of the Markets: Wars are equity bullish, but there's a catch...

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2022/02/wars-are-equity-bullish-but-theres-catch.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2022/02/wars-are-equity-bullish-but-theres-catch.html#0001-01-01)

Four weeks ago, I suggested that investors

[buy to the sound of cannons](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.com/2022/01/29/buy-to-the-sound-of-cannons/)

. Now that the cannons have sounded, is that still a good idea?

Yes, but there's a catch. A detailed list of past crises from

[Ed Clissold](https://twitter.com/edclissold/status/1496189092647874561)

of Ned Davis Research reveals that stock prices usually rebound strongly after sudden shocks such as war. On average, the DJIA is up 4.2% after a month and 15.3% a year later.

Here's the catch...
