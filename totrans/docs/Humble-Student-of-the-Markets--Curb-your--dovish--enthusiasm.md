# Humble Student of the Markets: Curb your (dovish) enthusiasm

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2018/12/curb-your-dovish-enthusiasm.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2018/12/curb-your-dovish-enthusiasm.html#0001-01-01)

**Mid-week market update**

: In the wake of the

[Powell speech](https://www.federalreserve.gov/newsevents/speech/powell20181128a.htm)

last week and the FOMC minutes, the market implied odds of rate hikes have plunged. While the base case calls for two rate hikes by June, the odds of a once and done policy after a December hike is rapidly increasing, while the probability of three more hikes by June has plunged.

Curb your dovish enthusiasm.
