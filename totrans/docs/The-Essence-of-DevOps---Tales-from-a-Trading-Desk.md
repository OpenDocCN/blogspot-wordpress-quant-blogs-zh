## The Essence of DevOps

NDC 2013 [offers](http://vimeo.com/ndcoslo/videos/sort:date/format:detail) amongst the n video’s, “Richard Campbell: People, Process, Tools – The [Essence](http://vimeo.com/68327402) of DevOps”.  If you have limited time, jump to 57:50 – allocate 20% of cycles to technical debt reduction before you end up with allocating 100%.  This one item is possibly the biggest take away from a video worth watching in full.  To many people in software (mostly management) believe software is an exact science that should incur zero debt – time to get real.
