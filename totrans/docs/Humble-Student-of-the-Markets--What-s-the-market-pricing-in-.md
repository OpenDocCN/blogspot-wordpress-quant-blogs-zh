# Humble Student of the Markets: What's the market pricing in?

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2022/01/whats-market-pricing-in.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2022/01/whats-market-pricing-in.html#0001-01-01)

As the stock market looks forward to another exciting week of volatility, the technical damage suffered by the market is quite severe. Nevertheless, investors need to take a deep breath and ask, "What's the market pricing in?"

The three major factors I consider in my analysis are:

*   Earnings and valuation;
*   Fed policy; and
*   Geopolitical risk.
