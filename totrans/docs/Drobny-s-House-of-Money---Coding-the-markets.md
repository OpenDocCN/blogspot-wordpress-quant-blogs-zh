## Drobny’s House of Money

### February 3, 2009

I’m reading [Steven Drobny](http://www.drobny.com/about.html)‘s [Inside the House of Money](http://books.global-investor.com/books/23076/Steven-Drobny/Inside-the-House-of-Money/). It’s an interesting mixture of anecdotage and trade ideas and discussion, together with a history of the hedge fund industry. Lots of good background on the Palindrome, Tiger, as well as detailed interviews with Jim Leitner and Christian Siva-Jothy. Recommended…
