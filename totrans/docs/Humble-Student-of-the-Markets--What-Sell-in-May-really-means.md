**Mid-week market update**

: Should you Sell in May and go away? While many traders are familiar with the Wall Street adage, what "Sell in May" really means is the six months starting May 1 has experienced subpar returns compared to the six months starting in November 1\. It's not necessarily bearish.

While it's not advisable to trade strictly on seasonality, investors might want to be extra cautious with their equity allocations this year.
