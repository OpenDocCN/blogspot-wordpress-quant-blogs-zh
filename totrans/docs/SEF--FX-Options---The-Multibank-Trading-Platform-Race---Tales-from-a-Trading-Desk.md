## SEF: FX Options – The Multibank Trading Platform Race

2012 saw a flurry of activity around multibank FX Option trading platforms which looks to [continue](http://www.fxweek.com/fx-week/news/2242413/surfacexchange-in-acquisition-talks) into 2013.  [FXall](http://www.fxall.com/about/news--press/8f91abe8-d548-4cd4-a2e5-155dbdf351db), [TradeWeb](http://www.tradeweb.com/Institutional/Derivatives/FX-Options/), GFI [Direct](http://www.fxweek.com/fx-week/news/2236603/gfi-fenics-repositions-fx-options-platform-as-it-targets-sef-licence) ([GFI Fenics](http://www.gfigroup.co.uk/gfifenics/products/fenicstrader.aspx)), [SurfacExchange](https://www.surfacexchange.com/Platform.aspx), and [Medusa](http://www.fxweek.com/fx-week/news/1899395/digital-vega-launches-multibank-options-trading-platform) ([Digital Vega](http://www.digitalvega.com/technology.html)).  Interesting, [Medusa](http://www.fxweek.com/fx-week/news/2242341/digital-vega-to-add-spot-to-medusa-options-platform) is integrated into the Traiana Harmony network.  The drive of this FX Options [activity](http://www.fxweek.com/fx-week/analysis/2158186/bank-focus-options-platforms-intensifies):  regulation 🙂 – specifically, swap execution facilities (Sef) under the US Dodd-Frank Act.
