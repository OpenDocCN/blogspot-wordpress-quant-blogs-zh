# Quantifiable Edges: Quick Note On Follow Through Day

> 来源：[http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2008/07/quick-note-on-follow-through-day.html#0001-01-01](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2008/07/quick-note-on-follow-through-day.html#0001-01-01)

Investors Business Daily did not declare Tuesday a Follow Through Day. It did qualify under the original 1% rules, though. I conducted most of my studies using the old 1% rules. One primary reason is that moving the requiremnt to a 1.7% move,

[several large rallies would have been missed](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2008/01/follow-through-days-pt-2-does-every.html)

. There are a couple of things to note when the market puts in an “Original Follow Through Day” (OFTD) like today. First, based on the 64 OFTD’s I found in my study between 1971 and January of 2008, the expectation over the next week was positive. Second, however the market moved over course of the next week was a predictor of the success or failure of the FTD about 2/3 of the time.

[You may refer here](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2008/02/short-term-implications-of-follow.html)

for more details on these statistics.
