# Quantifiable Edges: A Breadth Measure That Hit an Extreme Monday

> 来源：[http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2011/06/breadth-measure-that-hit-extreme-monday.html#0001-01-01](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2011/06/breadth-measure-that-hit-extreme-monday.html#0001-01-01)

To examine breadth one measure I use looks at the % rank of the % of up volume on a certain day over the last year. This reading is shown on the charts page every night. It is also part of the

[Tradestation package available for purchase (or free to subscribers).](http://quantifiableedges.blogspot.com/2011/05/new-tool-for-quantifiable-edges.html)

Monday’s Up Volume % breadth was among the lowest 2% of the last year. With the market also being down for the 4th day in a row, that triggered this study from the 11/17/10 subscriber letter.

I’m seeing lots of evidence suggesting a bounce is imminent. The last couple of days the market has not complied. I suspect it will fairly soon.
