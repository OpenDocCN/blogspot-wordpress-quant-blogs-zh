# VIX and More: Portfolio A1 Continues Solid Performance

Four of Portfolio A1’s five holdings – all but [WCG](http://finance.yahoo.com/q?s=wcg), which was purchased on Monday – can now claim double digit returns.  As WCG (WellCare Health Plans) has been a top performer in the red hot health care sector, the current composition of this portfolio looks as strong as it has been since the launch eight weeks ago.  During those 8 weeks, Portfolio has gained 4.4%, while the benchmark S&P 500 has declined 0.2%.

There are no changes to the portfolio for the coming week.
