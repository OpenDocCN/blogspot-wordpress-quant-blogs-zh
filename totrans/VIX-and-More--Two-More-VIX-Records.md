# VIX and More: Two More VIX Records

> 来源：[http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/10/two-more-vix-records.html#0001-01-01](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/10/two-more-vix-records.html#0001-01-01)

Once again, the VIX is in the record books, with two new highs:

*   new closing high: 69.95
*   new intra-day high: 76.94

Of course, today is also the first time the VIX traded over 70.

In other volatility index news, the

[VXO](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VXO)

(CBOE S&P 100 Volatility Index) closed at 86.14, almost exactly half of the all-time record high of 172.79 from October 20, 1987\. The VXO had an intra-day high of 103.18, marking the first time the 'original VIX' has been over the 100 mark since 1987.
