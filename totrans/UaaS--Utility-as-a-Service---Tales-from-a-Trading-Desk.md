## UaaS: Utility-as-a-Service

Market migrates to the new [UaaS](http://www.wbresearch.com/tradingarchitecture/home.aspx) (Utility-as-a-Service) paradigm – the new 4 letter cloud acronym

UaaS in the financial vertical is possibly defined as an industry wide utility for a service that offers functionality that is not a core differentiators for institutions, which from a price point delivers better value via a cross institution utility than institutions delivering their own solutions
