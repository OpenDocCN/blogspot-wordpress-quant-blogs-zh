**Mid-week market update**

: The bear market rally appears to have stalled at the first Fibonacci resistance level of 2650\. The bulls also failed to stage an upside breakout through the falling trend line. Instead, it broke down through the (dotted) rising trend line, indicating the bears had taken control of the tape.
