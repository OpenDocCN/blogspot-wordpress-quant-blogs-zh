## The maths of limit order book behaviour

### September 19, 2008

Lately I’ve been taking an interest in the maths of limit order book behaviour. Electronic exchanges, and many ECNs, are organised as [limit order book](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Order_(exchange)) trading systems. Stock exchanges, like the [LSE](http://www.londonstockexchange.com/en-gb/), and futures exchanges, like [Eurex](http://www.eurexchange.com/index.html), are organised as limit order books. [Larry Harris](http://www.tradingandexchanges.com) has interesting comments to make on the interplay between limit and market orders on limit order books.
