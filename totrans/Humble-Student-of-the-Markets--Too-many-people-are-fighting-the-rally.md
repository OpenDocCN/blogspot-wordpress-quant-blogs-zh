# Humble Student of the Markets: Too many people are fighting the rally

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2023/11/too-many-people-are-fighting-rally.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2023/11/too-many-people-are-fighting-rally.html#0001-01-01)

**Mid-week market update**: Until yesterday’s market melt-up, it seemed that individual investors were fighting the rally, which is a contrarian bullish sign. I noticed it on the weekend when the level of engagement on my bearish tweets were an order of magnitude higher than my bullish ones. In addition, the New York Fed’s recent [consumer expectations survey](https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/sce#/stockprice-1) was having trouble finding equity bulls.
