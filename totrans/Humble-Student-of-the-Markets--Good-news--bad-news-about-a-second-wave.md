**Mid-week market update**

: I have some good news, and bad news about a second wave. The bad news is new case counts are rising dramatically in the US. The good news is fatalities are not rising.

Here is some more bad news.

So before you start talking about what a second wave is, what we'd like to do is get this outbreak under control over the next couple of months."
