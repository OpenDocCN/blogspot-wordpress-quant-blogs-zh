# Humble Student of the Markets: Do the bulls have a sentiment problem?

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2020/11/do-bulls-have-sentiment-problem.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2020/11/do-bulls-have-sentiment-problem.html#0001-01-01)

**Mid-week market update**: (I am publishing my mid-week market update a day early owing to the US Thanksgiving holiday shortened week)

Should the bulls be worried? The [Greed and Fear Index](https://money.cnn.com/data/fear-and-greed/) has surged to 88, which represents a warning of excessive bullishness.

As well, [Willie Delwiche](https://twitter.com/WillieDelwiche/status/1330950412753661952) pointed out that his survey of sentiment indicators are all tilted either contrarian bearish or neutral. Delwiche concluded that "everyone knows about (& is positioned for) historical tendency for stocks to rally from here into year-end".
