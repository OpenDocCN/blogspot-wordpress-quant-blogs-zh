## Lords of Finance

### December 14, 2009

Got a copy of [Lords of Finance](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Lords-Finance-Great-Depression-Bankers/dp/0434015415) as a birthday present at the weekend. I’ve been enoying the opening chapters hugely. The author, Liaquat Ahmed, has done for financial history what Anthony Beevor did for WWII with [Stalingrad](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Stalingrad-Antony-Beevor/dp/0141032405). Explication of grand strategy is interwoven with human detail to create narrative momentum. Strongly recommended…
