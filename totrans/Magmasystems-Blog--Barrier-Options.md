# Magmasystems Blog: Barrier Options

> 来源：[http://magmasystems.blogspot.com/2006/08/barrier-options.html#0001-01-01](http://magmasystems.blogspot.com/2006/08/barrier-options.html#0001-01-01)

Barrier Options are considered to be exotic derivatives. Exotics usually take a long time to price, and if you are architecting a trading system, you usually want to put a lot of computing power behind the calculations of exotics. Many trading firms look to

[Compute Grids](http://www.wallstreetandtech.com/showArticle.jhtml?articleID=175802180)

and data caches to help speed up some of these calculations.
