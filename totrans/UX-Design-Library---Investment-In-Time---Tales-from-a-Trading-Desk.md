## UX Design Library – Investment In Time

eightshapes offer an interesting [PDF](http://eightshapesunify.s3.amazonaws.com/CreatingAUXDesignLibrary.Final.pdf) for “Creating a UX Deisgn Library”.  I particularly like the “How much should we invest in each part?” and the team vs recommend for Comps (pixel-perfect pages).  Not unsurprising, given that the PDF is about web design, and web design in a browser is never going to be pixel-perfect compared to an Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator image crafted by a designer.  Wire-frame and design in a browser is I believe the [take away](http://eightshapesunify.s3.amazonaws.com/CreatingAUXDesignLibrary.Final.pdf).
