# Humble Student of the Markets: Studies in market psychology: The Debate and Deutsche Bank

**Mid-week market update:**

From a trader`s perspective, this market had been jittery and mainly driven by two themes. The first was the resolution of the uncertainty over the presidential debate that occurred Monday night. The other is the uncertainty over the fate of Deutsche Bank as a symptom of the systemic risk posed by European financials.

The market reactions to these themes can hold clues to short-term market direction.
