# Humble Student of the Markets: A possible generational low in oil and energy stocks

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2016/01/a-possible-generational-low-in-oil-and.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2016/01/a-possible-generational-low-in-oil-and.html#0001-01-01)

The bad news just doesn't stop coming for oil. It all began when Saudi Arabia had turned on the production spigots to counter growing production from American frackers, and now it has to contend with the geopolitical dimensions of the growing power of Russia and Iran in the Middle East. The calls are growing for

[$20 oil](http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-01-12/-20-oil-no-longer-mirage-as-world-confronts-price-at-12-year-low)

and even

[$10 oil](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/finance/oilprices/12094394/Oil-price-could-fall-to-10-a-barrel-warn-investment-bank-bears.html)

as there seems to be no prospects of an end to the oversupplied market.
