# VIX and More: Portfolio A1 Begins 2008 By Increasing Gap on SPX

> 来源：[http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/01/portfolio-begins-2008-by-increasing-gap.html#0001-01-01](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/01/portfolio-begins-2008-by-increasing-gap.html#0001-01-01)

While the first three trading days of 2008 knocked 0.8% off of the value of Portfolio A1, this was much better than the 3.9% loss in the benchmark S&P 500 index.  Since the February 16, 2007 inception, Portfolio A1 now stands with a gain of 21%, in contrast to a 3% loss for the SPX.

[Brasil Telecom Participacoes S.A. (BRP)](http://finance.google.com/finance?q=brp) was the only stock in the portfolio that advanced during the week, bucking the downtrend in US-based technology stocks.
