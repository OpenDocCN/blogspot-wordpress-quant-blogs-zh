# VIX and More: VIX:VXV Ratio Approaches Bullish Territory

> 来源：[http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/07/vixvxv-ratio-approaches-bullish.html#0001-01-01](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2008/07/vixvxv-ratio-approaches-bullish.html#0001-01-01)

One of the better indicators over the past 7 ½ months has been the

[VIX:VXV ratio](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VIX%3AVXV)

, which, as the chart below shows, has been quite accurate in calling tops and bottoms in the market following the

[VXV](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VXV)

launch back in November 2007 and I whose

[application I pioneered](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2007/12/vixvxv-ratio.html)

soon thereafter. I mention this because the last time the VIX:VXV ratio gave a clear bullish signal was at the mid-March bottom; and the ratio is coming close to another VIX:VXV bullish signal today.
