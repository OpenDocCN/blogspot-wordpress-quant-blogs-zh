# Humble Student of the Markets: Why I tactically shorted stocks today

> 来源：[https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2016/02/why-i-tactically-shorted-stocks-today.html#0001-01-01](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2016/02/why-i-tactically-shorted-stocks-today.html#0001-01-01)

Regular readers recognize that my inner investor is bullish on stocks, though my inner trader remains a little leery and expects further market choppiness (see

[Profit by thinking like Big Money](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.com/2016/02/14/profit-by-thinking-like-big-money/)

). Despite my longer term bullishness, I

[tweeted](https://twitter.com/HumbleStudent/status/699980289099956227)

this morning that I had tactically taken a small, underline the word "small", short position in SPX in my trading account.

I did it for the following reasons:

*   The market was due for a bounce - and bounce it did;
*   The market moved quickly from oversold to overbought; and
*   The technical structure of market action is a bearish structure.
