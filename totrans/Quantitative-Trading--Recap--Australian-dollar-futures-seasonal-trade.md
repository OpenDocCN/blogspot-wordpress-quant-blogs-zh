# Quantitative Trading: Recap: Australian dollar futures seasonal trade

> 来源：[http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/05/recap-australian-dollar-futures.html#0001-01-01](http://epchan.blogspot.com/2007/05/recap-australian-dollar-futures.html#0001-01-01)

Yesterday was the exit of the Australian dollar futures seasonal trade which I discussed in my

[premium content](http://www.epchan.com/subscriptions.html)

. It incurred a loss of $920 per contract, despite a 12-year winning streak previously. This may be the peril of a trade that is not based on any fundamental rationale that I know of, as well as an in-sample bias that I alluded to in my previous article. I will keep it on my watchlist for another year.
