# VIX and More: ISEE Highlights Froth

> 来源：[http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2007/10/isee-highlights-froth.html#0001-01-01](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2007/10/isee-highlights-froth.html#0001-01-01)

![](img/95d76bbd136a4c156f74ab18d713d555.png)Just in case it is not already obvious to anyone who may be an occasional reader, the VIX and the ISEE are my two favorite measures of market sentiment.  

Right now the VIX is suggesting that the current market environment is overheated.  This is evident in measures such as the distance the VIX is below various moving averages, the [VWSI](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VWSI), and the [VIX:SDS](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VIX%3ASDS) ratio.

Until recently, the ISEE was a little more prone to fence-sitting, but that changed with yesterday’s 187 reading, the highest single day reading since August 2006.  On the heels of that large number comes a wave of call buying this morning that has the [ISEE at 270](http://www.ise.com/WebForm/viewPage.aspx?categoryId=126&header3=true&menu0=true&link1=true) as of 11:10 EDT.  Now it is not unusual to see extreme readings in the ISEE early in the day, when the denominator is low, but what is unusual is to see those extreme readings get even more extreme as the day wears on, such as the ISEE actually jumping up from 257 to 270 during the last 40 minutes.  This development bears watching…
