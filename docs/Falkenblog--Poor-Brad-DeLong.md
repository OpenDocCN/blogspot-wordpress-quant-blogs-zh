# Falkenblog: Poor Brad DeLong

> 来源：[`falkenblog.blogspot.com/2011/08/poor-brad-delong.html#0001-01-01`](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2011/08/poor-brad-delong.html#0001-01-01)

Über Keynesian Bradley DeLong seems pretty unhappy in

[Bloggingheads.tv diavlog](http://bloggingheads.tv/diavlogs/37836)

, just as he does in his blog posts. In this clip he gratuitously slams

[liberaltarian](http://falkenblog.blogspot.com/2011/03/remember-liberaltarianism.html)

Brink Lindsey for not being an economist and spouting economic opinions, as if he would apply this qualification to anyone who agrees with his assumptions (eg, Obama, Matt Yglesias, Ezra Klein), or that macroeconomists have demonstrated any reason for deference on these matters:

当我心情不好时，我会尽量避免和人交谈或写作，因为我没办法有效地得到我想要的东西。很显然他心情不好，也许是因为他没有被邀请以某种重要的身份加入奥巴马政府，尽管从他的表现来看，这只证明了这是一个明智的决定。
