# Humble Student of the Markets: The erosion of American competitive advantage

> 来源：[`humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2010/07/erosion-of-american-competitive.html#0001-01-01`](https://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2010/07/erosion-of-american-competitive.html#0001-01-01)

新闻闪电般传到我的桌面上：

[中国领先的信用评级机构将美国、英国和法国从 AAA 降级](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/finance/china-business/7886077/Chinese-rating-agency-strips-Western-nations-of-AAA-status.html)

.这无疑是一个即将到来的事情的信号。问题是，美国能保持其作为领先经济大国的地位吗？

The news is grim. I have written before about the

[deficit reduction analytical framework](http://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2010/06/analytical-framework-for-deficit-hawks.html)

.那种宏观经济调整只有在美国人保持其根本的竞争优势时才有效。

[约翰·赫斯曼](http://www.hussmanfunds.com/wmc/wmc100712.htm)

wrote this week that the basis of American competitive advantage rests on superior physical capital and human capital [emphasis added]:

> The main source of this difference in productivity is that U.S. workers have a substantially larger stock of productive capital per worker, as well as generally higher levels of educational attainment, which is a form of human capital. This relative abundance of physical and educational capital has been a driver of U.S. prosperity for generations. ***Neither advantage in capital, however, is intrinsic to American workers, and it will be impossible to prevent a long-term convergence of U.S. wages toward those of developing countries unless the U.S. efficiently allocates its resources to productive investment and educational quality.*** This is where our policy makers are failing us.

在过去的几十年里，美国在两个领域都浪费了其领先地位。我们没有投资于生产性实物资本，却看到了过度的错误投资导致了科技、房地产和金融领域的泡沫。互联网和房地产泡沫是显而易见的。

至于金融业，所有将人才错误投资于华尔街的事实向世界表明了什么？尽管有

[金融监管的无效努力](http://noir.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601087&sid=aclRkBNnumYo&)

, American remain in denial about the role of finance in society. The news of the

[艾伦·格林斯潘在纽约大学的职位](http://www.ritholtz.com/blog/2010/07/greenspan-chair-at-nyu/)

just another sign of denial.

‘Nuff said.

首先，教育的领先地位不再像过去那样。最近的一项研究得出结论，

[精英大学正在侵蚀其竞争优势](http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6VBX-4WB3NGH-2&_user=10&_rdoc=1&_fmt=&_orig=search&_sort=d&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=781afa4b3214225b11556dc05e37006d)

。我有

[博客文章](http://humblestudentofthemarkets.blogspot.com/2009/06/rorschbach-tests.html)

关于一个天安门广场抗议者因加拿大教育系统质量较低，而决定带着孩子回到中国，这个系统从第一手经验来看与美国系统相当或略高。

[Rolfe Winkler](http://blogs.reuters.com/rolfe-winkler/2009/09/04/extra-credit-could-bankrupt-students/)
