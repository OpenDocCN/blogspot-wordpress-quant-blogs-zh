[VIX:VXV 比率](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VIX%3AVXV)

[VXV](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/search/label/VXV)

[我首创的应用程序](http://vixandmore.blogspot.com/2007/12/vixvxv-ratio.html)
